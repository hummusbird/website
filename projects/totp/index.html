introduction

RFC-6238 is the spec which defines how to generate those 6 digit numbers on your phone - the ones you use to log into your Microsoft account for the seventh time that day

although those numbers seem to come out of nowhere, the algorithm is very simple, easy to implement, and easier to crack!
