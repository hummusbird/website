the plan:

since the stalks on my Corsair K68 RGB started to wear out, i've been wanting a new keyboard, preferrably handmade, and without a numberpad for extra mouse space.

i couldn't find any premade PCBs in this layout for not a stupid amount of money, and handwiring my own was a much cheaper option than getting PCBs cut.

hardware:

my first handmade keyboard was shit - it used gateron blues, a 3D printed chassis [sick68] and a teensy 2.0. i forgot to buy stabilizers, but the concept was sound and worked.

  • teensy 2.0
  • 3D printed custom chassis
  • stripped ethernet cable
  • costar stabilizers
  • susuwatari MT3 caps
  • USB mini B cable
