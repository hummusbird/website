the disk

recently at work i've had to use floppy disks - the 3.5 inch, 1.44 MB plastic rectangles from the 80s. younger people might never have even used one of these disks, which were so ubiquitous at their peak that we STILL use a floppy disk to represent the "save" icon in thousands of GUIs.
